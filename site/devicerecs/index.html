    Best Daily Carry (EDC)

    If you are looking for a reliable, rugged, and water-resistant node with a 5-day battery life, the T1000-E from Seeed Studio is a great choice. You can purchase it directly from Seeed Studio with 5% coupon K7ABUUV9 or on Amazon.

    Solar Node Recommendations

    Pre-made Solar Nodes

    If you prefer a "plug-and-play" solution that is weather-ready out of the box, the SenseCAP P1 or P1 Pro from Seeed Studio are excellent choices. These units integrate the solar controller and enclosure, saving you significant assembly time. you can recieve a 5% discount by code S9ZRMA1X at checkout.

    The P1 is $35 cheaper as it does not include batteries or GPS; you will need to supply four 18650 batteries yourself. By comparison, the P1 Pro includes everything you need to get started, including an integrated GPS module.
